# Koine Greek Intermediate Course (Comprehensive & Exhaustive)

## Module Overview
This intermediate module expands grammar, syntax, verbs, participles, adjectives, cases, and translation technique.

## Lesson 1 — Review & Expansion of Alphabet, Vowels, Diphthongs
Full pronunciation, breathing marks, accents, iota subscript.

## Lesson 2 — Eight Case Functions
Nominative, Genitive, Dative, Accusative, Vocative, + extended uses (subjective genitive, objective genitive, dative of means, etc.)

## Lesson 3 — Second & Third Declension Mastery
Consonant stems, liquid stems, irregular nouns.

## Lesson 4 — Adjectives
Three‑ending, two‑ending, one‑ending Greek adjectives; attributive vs predicate position.

## Lesson 5 — Pronouns
Personal, demonstrative, interrogative, relative pronouns.

## Lesson 6 — Prepositions
With each case, spatial meaning, metaphorical meaning, advanced usage chart.

## Lesson 7 — Verb System Deep Dive
Tense, voice, mood; full indicative, participles, infinitives; overview of non‑indicative moods.

## Lesson 8 — Present, Imperfect, Future, Aorist Systems
Regular and irregular forms.

## Lesson 9 — Middle & Passive Voice
Deponent verbs, proper translation principles.

## Lesson 10 — Participles (The Heart of Greek Syntax)
Present, aorist, perfect forms; adverbial, adjectival, genitive absolute.

## Lesson 11 — Infinitives
Articular vs bare infinitives, purpose, result.

## Lesson 12 — Clauses & Conjunctions
ὅτι clauses, ἵνα clauses, conditional clauses, temporal clauses.

## Lesson 13 — Translation Techniques
Word order, identifying the main verb, clause chaining.

## Lesson 14 — Intermediate Vocabulary (350 Words)
Sorted by semantic domain.

## Lesson 15 — Manuscript Reading Practice
Long sentences with full parsing and translation walkthrough.

## Lesson 16 — Intermediate Quizzes
Parsing drills, identification, translation.

## Lesson 17 — Final Exam
40 parsing questions, 10 translations, 20 vocabulary items, 5 clause analyses.

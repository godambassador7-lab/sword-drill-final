# Koine Greek Advanced Course (Exhaustive Master-Level)

## Module Overview
This advanced module trains mastery of syntax, discourse, verbal aspect, advanced participles, sentence diagramming, textual criticism, and manuscript reading from the Septuagint and Codex Sinaiticus.

## Lesson 1 — Verbal Aspect Theory
Perfective, imperfective, stative aspects; Aktionsart; pragmatic vs semantic aspect.

## Lesson 2 — Advanced Tenses
Aorist subcategories, gnomic aorist, dramatic aorist, historical presents, perfect periphrastics, pluperfect nuance.

## Lesson 3 — Voice System Deep Analysis
Middle-only verbs, passive-only verbs, direct vs indirect middle, reflexive nuance, idiomatic middles.

## Lesson 4 — Advanced Syntax of Participles
Genitive absolute deep dive, supplementary participles, imperatival participles, conditional participles.

## Lesson 5 — Subjunctive Mood Mastery
Purpose, result, conditional, hortatory, deliberative, prohibitions, μή vs οὐ distinctions.

## Lesson 6 — Optative Mood (Rare but Critical)
Potential, wish, concluding clauses, historical sequence usage.

## Lesson 7 — Conditional Sentence System
First, second, third, fourth class conditions; εἰ vs ἐάν; contrary-to-fact nuance.

## Lesson 8 — Discourse Analysis
Theme development, background vs foreground, sentence flow, verbal prominence, narrative peaks.

## Lesson 9 — Advanced Preposition Usage
Metaphorical meaning charts, idiomatic exceptions, case switching.

## Lesson 10 — Advanced Clausal Structures
ὅτι clauses revisited, ἵνα nuance, causal clauses, concessive clauses, temporal clauses.

## Lesson 11 — Sentence Diagramming (Greek Style)
Clause chains, embedded structures, modifiers, verbal centers, subject tracking.

## Lesson 12 — Intermediate-to-Advanced Vocabulary (500 Words)
Semantic domains: theology, philosophy, daily life, compound verbs.

## Lesson 13 — Septuagint Manuscript Reading
Isaiah, Psalms, Genesis reading walkthroughs with full parsing.

## Lesson 14 — Codex Sinaiticus NT Reading
John, Romans, Hebrews passage breakdown; nomina sacra recognition.

## Lesson 15 — Textual Criticism Basics
Uncials, minuscules, papyri, variant families, critical apparatus reading.

## Lesson 16 — Parsing Master Drills
40 verbs, 40 nouns, 20 pronouns, 15 participles, 10 infinitives.

## Lesson 17 — Final Master Exam
Translation of 8 advanced passages, 50 parsing questions, 10 discourse analyses, 5 textual apparatus readings.

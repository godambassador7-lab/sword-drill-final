# New Moons (Rosh Chodesh)

New Moons mark the beginning of each month in the biblical calendar.

## 1. Biblical Foundations

Various passages mention:

- Trumpet blasts at New Moon.
- Additional offerings in the sanctuary.
- New Moons as appointed times.

## 2. Function in Israel's Life

New Moons:

- Oriented the people to God's ordering of time.
- Marked transitions between months and prepared the way for larger feasts.

## 3. Participating Today

Ways to remember New Moons:

- Note the beginning of each biblical month and briefly reflect or pray.
- Use it as a moment to reset intentions and remember that time itself is a gift.

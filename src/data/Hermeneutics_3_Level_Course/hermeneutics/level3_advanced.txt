1. Intertextuality
How later biblical authors quote or echo earlier texts.
Example: Matthew quoting Hosea / Isaiah.

2. Biblical Theology vs Systematic Theology
Biblical: Traces themes through Scripture.
Systematic: Organizes doctrines by topic.

3. Discourse Analysis
Look for:
- Repeated words
- Structural markers
- Inclusio
- Chiasm patterns

4. Advanced Genre Study
Apocalyptic literature:
- Symbolic numbers
- Vision sequences
- Heavenly court scenes

5. Resolving Difficult Texts
Process:
1. Identify genre
2. Examine context layers
3. Consider linguistic possibilities
4. Compare parallel passages
5. Evaluate cultural assumptions

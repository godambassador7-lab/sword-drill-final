# Church History & Bible Culture Course

## Overview
A comprehensive three-level course covering biblical culture, ancient customs, and church history from the Old Testament through the early church period.

## Course Structure

### Beginner Level: Foundations of Biblical Culture
**Requirements:** Unlocked by default
**Completion:** Complete 5 lessons + pass certification exam

#### Lessons:
1. **Introduction to the Biblical World** (20 min)
   - Geography of the Ancient Near East
   - Time periods and regions
   - Climate and agriculture
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

2. **Daily Life in Ancient Israel** (25 min)
   - Family structure and roles
   - Housing, food, clothing
   - Occupations and trades
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

3. **The Biblical Calendar & Feast Days** (30 min)
   - Hebrew calendar system
   - Seven major feasts
   - Sabbath observance
   - Agricultural and religious significance
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

4. **Worship & the Tabernacle** (25 min)
   - Tabernacle design and symbolism
   - Sacrificial system
   - Priesthood and Levites
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

5. **Marriage & Family in Biblical Times** (20 min)
   - Betrothal and wedding customs
   - Family roles and inheritance
   - Education of children
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

6. **Foundations Certification Exam** (30 min)
   - **15 comprehensive questions**
   - Covers all beginner topics
   - 70% passing score required
   - Unlocks Intermediate level

---

### Intermediate Level: Temple, Tradition & Early Church
**Requirements:** Complete beginner level (5 lessons)
**Completion:** Complete 6 lessons + pass certification exam

#### Lessons:
1. **The Second Temple Period** (30 min)
   - From Exile to Roman rule
   - Rebuilding the Temple
   - Religious groups: Pharisees, Sadducees, Essenes, Zealots
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

2. **Synagogue & Jewish Education** (25 min)
   - Origin and purpose of synagogues
   - Sabbath services and Torah reading
   - Three-level education system
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

3. **Jesus in His Cultural Context** (35 min)
   - Nazareth and Galilean culture
   - Jesus as rabbi and teacher
   - Parables and agricultural imagery
   - Jewish expectations of Messiah
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

4. **Biblical Festivals & Jesus** (30 min)
   - Jesus and Passover - Last Supper
   - Pentecost and the Holy Spirit
   - Feast of Tabernacles
   - Hanukkah encounter
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

5. **Birth of the Church (Acts 1-12)** (35 min)
   - Pentecost and the Holy Spirit
   - Jerusalem church growth
   - Stephen's martyrdom
   - Gospel spreads to Gentiles
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

6. **Paul's Missionary Journeys** (30 min)
   - Conversion of Saul to Paul
   - Three missionary journeys
   - Church planting strategy
   - Letters to the churches
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

7. **Temple & Church Certification Exam** (45 min)
   - **20 comprehensive questions**
   - Covers all intermediate topics
   - 70% passing score required
   - Unlocks Advanced level

---

### Advanced Level: Early Church History & Development
**Requirements:** Complete intermediate level (6 lessons)
**Completion:** Complete 7 lessons + pass mastery exam

#### Lessons:
1. **The Apostolic Fathers** (35 min)
   - Clement, Ignatius, Polycarp
   - The Didache
   - Shepherd of Hermas
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

2. **Persecution & Martyrdom** (30 min)
   - Roman persecution under various emperors
   - Reasons for persecution
   - Famous martyrs
   - Edict of Milan (313 AD)
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

3. **Early Heresies & Orthodoxy** (40 min)
   - Gnosticism, Marcionism, Montanism, Arianism
   - Church's response: creeds and canon
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

4. **The Ecumenical Councils** (40 min)
   - Council of Nicaea (325) - deity of Christ
   - Council of Constantinople (381) - Trinity
   - Council of Ephesus (431)
   - Council of Chalcedon (451) - two natures of Christ
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

5. **The Church Fathers & Theology** (35 min)
   - Justin Martyr, Irenaeus, Tertullian
   - Origen, Augustine, Jerome
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

6. **Monasticism & Early Worship** (30 min)
   - Desert fathers
   - Benedict and Benedictine Rule
   - Early liturgy development
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

7. **Biblical Geography Deep Dive** (35 min)
   - Israel's geography
   - Paul's missionary routes
   - Seven churches of Revelation
   - Archaeological discoveries
   - **Quiz:** 5 questions

8. **Church History Mastery Certification** (60 min)
   - **25 comprehensive questions**
   - Covers all advanced topics
   - 70% passing score required
   - Course completion!

## Quiz Features

### For Regular Lessons:
- 5 multiple-choice/true-false questions per lesson
- Immediate feedback with explanations
- 60% passing score
- Can retake to improve score
- Progress tracking throughout quiz

### For Certification Exams:
- Beginner: 15 questions
- Intermediate: 20 questions
- Advanced: 25 questions
- 70% passing score required
- Comprehensive coverage of level material
- Must pass to unlock next level
- Detailed performance breakdown
- Can retake if needed

### Quiz Interface:
- Clean, intuitive design
- Progress bar showing advancement
- Question counter
- Instant answer feedback
- Detailed explanations for each question
- Score tracking during quiz
- Final results with performance analysis
- Option to retry or review material

## Progression System

1. **Beginner Level** - Always unlocked
2. **Intermediate Level** - Unlocked after completing 5 beginner lessons
3. **Advanced Level** - Unlocked after completing 6 intermediate lessons
4. **Lessons** - Each lesson unlocks after completing the previous one
5. **Certification** - Pass exam to fully complete a level

## Learning Outcomes

By completing this course, students will:

### Biblical Culture Knowledge:
- Understand daily life, customs, and festivals in ancient Israel
- Know the Tabernacle/Temple worship system
- Comprehend marriage, family, and social structures
- Grasp the biblical calendar and its significance

### Second Temple & Jesus' Context:
- Understand Second Temple Judaism
- Know the major Jewish groups and their beliefs
- See how Jesus fulfilled feast day symbolism
- Comprehend the cultural context of the Gospels

### Early Church History:
- Trace the birth and growth of the church
- Understand the Apostolic Fathers' role
- Know major heresies and orthodox responses
- Comprehend the ecumenical councils' importance
- Appreciate the Church Fathers' contributions
- Understand early monasticism and worship development

## Technical Details

### Quiz Question Format:
```json
{
  "id": 1,
  "question": "Question text here?",
  "options": [
    "Option A",
    "Option B",
    "Option C",
    "Option D"
  ],
  "correct": 0,  // Index of correct answer (0-based)
  "explanation": "Detailed explanation of the correct answer"

```

### Integration:
- Course data: `courseData` from `course_content.json`
- Quiz data: `quizData` from `quiz_content.json`
- Quiz component: `ChurchHistoryQuiz` handles all quiz logic
